About Olive & Thyme

We believe that great cooking should not be intimidating, inaccessible, or reserved for professionals. Olive & Thyme was created to bring the world's most exciting flavors directly into your home kitchen with recipes that are meticulously tested, clearly explained, and genuinely achievable for cooks of every skill level.

Our recipes travel the globe — from the spice-filled souks of Morocco to the street-food stalls of Bangkok, from the rustic kitchens of Tuscany to the izakayas of Tokyo. Every recipe is developed from scratch, tested multiple times in a real home kitchen, and written with extraordinary detail so that nothing is left to guesswork.

We explain not just what to do, but why — because understanding the science and technique behind each step makes you a fundamentally better, more confident cook who can adapt, improvise, and create with freedom.
